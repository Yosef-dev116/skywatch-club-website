Welcome

Skywatch Club is a student-friendly group that meets monthly to learn constellations, share telescope tips, and plan night-sky photo walks.

Quick links: visit our gallery, check the meetup plan, or sign up.

What we do

  • Beginner constellation tours
  • Moon + planet viewing nights
  • Photo sharing and critique

How a night works

  1. Meet at the location 20 minutes before sunset
  2. Safety check: flashlights, warm layers, battery
  3. Observe targets and take photos
